== IN1.19 - Insured's Address (XAD)

[segment-definition]
This field contains the address of the insured person. The insured is the person who has an agreement with the insurance company to provide healthcare services to persons covered by an insurance policy. Multiple addresses for the same insured person may be in this field. As of v 2.7, no assumptions can be made based on position or sequence. Specification of meaning based on sequence is deprecated.
