== IN2.38 - Student Indicator (CWE)

[segment-definition]
This field identifies whether the insured is currently a student or not, and whether the insured is a full-time or a part-time student. This field does not indicate the degree level (high school, college) of student, or his/her field of study (accounting, engineering, etc.). Refer to {{v2-table:0231:Student Status}} in Chapter 2C, Code Tables, for suggested values.
