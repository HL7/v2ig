== ORG.12 - Primary Care Physician Indicator (ID)

[segment-definition]
This field contains an indicator for whether this practice specialty may act as a primary care physician (PCP). Refer to {{v2-table:0136:Yes/no Indicator}} for valid values.

[segment-comment]
Y this practice specialty may act as a primary care physician

N this practice specialty may not act as a primary care physician
