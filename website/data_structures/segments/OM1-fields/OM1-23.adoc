== OM1.23 - Typical Turn-Around Time (NM)

[segment-definition]
This field contains the typical processing time for single test/observation. This field indicates the time from the delivery of a specimen or transport of a patient to a diagnostic service and the completion of the study. It includes the usual waiting time. The units are measured in minutes.

[segment-comment]
*Note: This field is deprecated and retained for backward compatibility as of v 2.8.2. See OM1-57.*
