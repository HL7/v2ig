== OBR.48 - Medically Necessary Duplicate Procedure Reason (CWE)

[segment-definition]
This field is used to document why the procedure found in _OBR-44 - Procedure Code_ is a duplicate of one ordered/charged previously for the same patient within the same date of service and has been determined to be medically necessary. The reason may be coded or it may be a free text entry.

[segment-comment]
This field is intended to provide financial systems information on who to bill for duplicate procedures.

Refer to {{v2-table:0476:Medically Necessary Duplicate Procedure Reason}} in Chapter 2C, Code Tables, for suggested values.
