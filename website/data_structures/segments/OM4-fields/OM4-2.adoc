== OM4.2 - Derived Specimen (ID)

[segment-definition]
This field contains the codes that identify the parents and children for diagnostic studies—especially in microbiology—where the initial specimen (e.g., blood) is processed to produce results (e.g., the identity of the bacteria grown out of the culture). The process also produces new "specimens" (e.g., pure culture of staphylococcus, and E. coli), and these are studied by a second order process (bacterial sensitivities).
