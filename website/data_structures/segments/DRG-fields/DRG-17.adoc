== DRG.17 - Status Patient (CWE)

[segment-definition]
This field contains the status of the patient concerning the financial aspects. It indicates whether the length of stay is normal or respectively shorter or longer than normal. Refer to {{v2-table:0739:DRG Status Patient}} in Chapter 2C, Code Tables, for suggested values.

[segment-comment]
This field is also used along with DRG-23 and DRG-24 as an indication of whether a surcharge (long length of stay) or a discount (short length of stay) has been determined.
